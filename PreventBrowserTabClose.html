Prevent Browser from Closing All Tabs

PURPOSE: This is a simple page that is designed to display a warning when you attempt to close the page. The idea is to leave this tab open in your browser so that your browser will display a warning before you attempt to close a browser window containing multiple tabs. This is a helpful way to prevent accidentally closing all tabs by closing a browser window.

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Click the button above or anywhere on this page to activate the features of this page. (Nothing will appear to happen when you click the button or click elsewhere on this page, but some browsers require you to click somewhere on a page before the page is permitted to display a warning when you attempt to close the page.)

    Note: If you close your browser while this tab is open and then later open the browser and this page is reloaded (such as if you click the "Remember my open pages" option in your browser, then you will need to click again to re-activate this page.

  2. Leave this tab open in your browser (and perhaps pin it as the first tab).
